Okay, so we've got our grilled cheese ingredients, but what about the steps required to make a grilled cheese?
Steps to a recipe need to be in order, otherwise we may end up with burnt cheese covered in bread and topped with a square of butter. The steps to making a basic grilled cheese would be:

  • Spread butter on bread and frying pan.
  • Place bread in frying pan and fry.
  • Add cheese on top of bread.
  • Cover with second slice of bread.
  • Turn over and fry for 2 minutes.
